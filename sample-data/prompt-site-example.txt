=== Site-Specific Configuration ===

Rename this file to data/prompt-<your-site>.txt and customise it for your
deployment. Any file matching data/prompt-*.txt is automatically appended
to the base system prompt.

Example: prompt-my-house.txt, prompt-workshop.txt, prompt-office.txt

Below is an example for an apartment. Adapt the layout, room names, and
naming conventions to match your space.

=== Apartment Layout ===

The apartment is called "My-Home" and is the top-level location (#1).

All rooms are nested under it. Each room has a single-letter prefix used for
naming furniture and sub-locations within that room.

My-Home (#1)               -- Top-level
+-- Living Room (#2)       [L-xxx]
+-- Bedroom (#3)           [B-xxx]
+-- Kitchen (#4)           [K-xxx]
+-- Bathroom (#5)          [T-xxx]
+-- Garage (#6)            [G-xxx]

Furniture naming convention: {prefix}-{nn} {name}
  e.g. K-01 Fridge, L-01 Bookshelf, G-03 Workbench

When creating sub-locations (furniture, shelves, drawers), nest them under the
appropriate room and follow the prefix convention:
  My-Home > Kitchen > K-01 Fridge
  My-Home > Living Room > L-01 Bookshelf > Shelf 2
